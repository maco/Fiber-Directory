Have you ever considered a garment made locally from fibers grown locally? If you want to make such a thing happen, you can find locally-grown fibers, weavers or knitters to turn it into fabric, and tailors to sew it. At least, that's the theory. This site is new, so if you have suggestions for additions to the database, please contact us!
